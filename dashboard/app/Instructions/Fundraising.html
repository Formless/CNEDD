Adding monthly data
  1. Click the "Fundraising Data" tab in the navigation bar
  2. Click "New Fundraising Month" from the actions panel
  3. Fill in the form with the year, month, and the rest of the data
  4. Click "Submit"
  5. You can now view, edit, or analyze the data

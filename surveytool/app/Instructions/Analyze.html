Statistics for one training
  1. Click the “Trainings” tab in the navigation bar
  2. Click on the name of the training for which you want survey statistics
  3. Click on "View Analysis" in the action panel
Statistics for a range of trainings
  1. Click the “Analysis” tab in the navigation bar
  2. Use the various fields to filter the surveys according to name, type, instructor, location, and date
  3. Click "Analyze"
How to use Charts page
  • The charts page shows various charts and graphs displaying statistics about surveys
  • Click on the titles of the graphs to expand or contract the "accordion"
  • You can print or download the individual charts by using the two icons in the top-right corner of each graph

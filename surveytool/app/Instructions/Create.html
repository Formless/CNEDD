Start new training
  1. Click the “Trainings” tab in the navigation bar
  2. Click “New Training” in the actions panel
  3. Fill in the form with the name, type, location, instructor, and date of the training
  4. Customize the 3 free response questions if you wish
  5. Click submit to save the training
Generate the survey
  1. Click the “Trainings” tab in the navigation bar
  2. Click on the name of the training for which you want to generate a survey
  3. Click "Generate PDF" in the action panel
  4. Your browser will download the survey PDF, which you can save or print
